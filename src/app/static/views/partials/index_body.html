Welcome to HABDash!

HABDash is a platfom for visualising data from your High Altitude Balloon flights. HABDash provides a simple api for uploading your flight data and gives you power to configure how that data shoould be presented.

You can add, remove and edit your flights through your dashboard, and many more featrues are coming soon!

Each flight has its own dedicated page which can be configured with a single json file.
The platform is design to scale well on mobile devices, so you can monitor your flights in the fields and on the go.

First time here?

First time here? Creating your flight only takes 5 minutes! Follow the get started guide to get started.
